Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my
top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:
  1. Salty's
  2. Cristiano's
  3. Kamakazee's
  4. Himawari
  5. D'Elegance Café
Salty's | Garapan, Saipan

Salty's serves curry with different kinds of proteins. Those are my favorite dishes that they serve. I never knew about Salty's even existed until my brother brought me there once. Now I like to introduce the restaurant to my friends.
Salty's became one of my favorite restaurants due to its great food, as well as the ocean view you get while dining there.

What I love:

  • Salty's pork katsu curry
  • Salty's spam katsu curry
  • Salty's ocean view
